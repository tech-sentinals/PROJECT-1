Common Heart Problems

High Blood Pressure

One of the leading causes of heart diseases. Early diagnosis is key.

Coronary Artery Disease

This condition occurs when the arteries supplying blood to the heart become narrow.

Arrhythmia

Irregular heartbeats can be a sign of an underlying condition.

Healthy Heart Practices

  • Regular Exercise - 30 minutes of moderate exercise a day.
  • Healthy Diet - Focus on fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Stress Management - Incorporate relaxation techniques.
  • Avoid Smoking - Significantly reduce the risk of heart disease.
